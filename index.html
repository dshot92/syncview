Shape vs Overlay

  • Blue shapes (reference): Editable by dragging individual points
  • Yellow shapes (overlay): Move entire shape as a unit
  • Both shapes: Fully draggable and movable
  • Adding Points: Click on the middle of a line to add new points
  • Deleting Points: Right-click on any point to delete it
